## Executive Summary

### What is Warehouse Intelligence Engine?

Warehouse Intelligence Engine is an AI-powered SaaS platform that transforms warehouse operations from reactive firefighting to proactive intelligence. Built by warehouse professionals who understand the real struggles of daily operations, the platform automatically analyzes inventory reports and detects operational anomalies before they become costly emergencies.

**The Core Problem We Solve:**
Inventory supervisors arrive at work, immediately run inventory analysis, and spend hours manually scanning Excel files to identify potential pallet losses, capacity issues, and operational inefficiencies. They hunt down suspicious pallets before "last minute panic calls" arrive. This manual process is time-consuming, error-prone, and exhausting.

**Our Solution:**
What takes 4-8 hours manually now takes 2 minutes with intelligent automation. Upload your inventory report, and our AI-powered rule engine identifies stagnant pallets, overcapacity situations, lot stragglers, invalid locations, and dozens of other issuesâ€”prioritized by severity and actionable immediately.

## Product Overview

### What Does It Do?

Warehouse Intelligence Engine is a comprehensive anomaly detection platform that:

1. **Analyzes Inventory Reports** - Upload Excel/CSV files with your daily inventory data
2. **Applies Intelligent Rules** - Dynamic rule engine evaluates data against configurable business rules
3. **Detects Anomalies** - Identifies operational issues across 9+ anomaly types
4. **Prioritizes Issues** - Categorizes problems by severity (Very High, High, Medium, Low)
5. **Provides Actionable Insights** - Clear, visual dashboards with location-specific recommendations
6. **Tracks Resolution** - Workflow management for anomaly resolution and team coordination

### Core Anomaly Types Detected

**Flow & Time Issues:**
- Stagnant pallets in receiving areas (>10 hours)
- Incomplete lots (pallets left behind when lot moves)
- Stuck pallets in transitional aisles
- Time-based workflow inefficiencies

**Space Management Issues:**
- Overcapacity violations (locations exceeding capacity limits)
- Invalid locations (pallets in undefined locations)
- Location mapping errors
- Space utilization problems

**Data Integrity Issues:**
- Scanner errors and duplicate scans
- Impossible location scenarios
- Data consistency problems
- Format validation issues
